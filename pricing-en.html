Pricing Policy

Standard Rate

My standard rate is 600 UAH per hour.

For long-term projects exceeding 50 hours, the rate can be reduced to 500 UAH per hour upon agreement.

Support Packages

  • 3 hours per month – 1500 UAH
  • 4 hours per month – 2000 UAH

Fixed monthly fee, regardless of actual task volume. Guaranteed priority within the allocated package hours.

Consultation & Estimation

Initial consultation and task evaluation — free of charge.

Work Format

Work is done in an iterative model: after receiving the task from the client, the first stage is completed, results approved, and then we proceed to the next iteration. After each completed stage, partial payment is made based on actual time spent.

The total time includes client support: explanation, solution demonstration, answering questions. This is part of the service and part of the value you receive along with the final result.
